Application Process

Step-by-Step USA Process

01

Counselling & Profile Review

We assess your academic background, English level, budget, goals, and preferred program.

02

University or College Selection

Shortlist suitable institutions based on course, location, tuition, ranking, and entry criteria.

03

Application Preparation

Prepare academic documents, English scores, recommendation letters, essays, and application forms.

04

Offer Letter & I-20

After admission, students receive the required documents for the student visa process.

05

Visa Preparation

Prepare financial documents, SEVIS, DS-160, interview preparation, and visa appointment guidance.

06

Pre-departure Support

Get guidance on travel, accommodation, airport arrival, insurance, and student life in the USA.

Requirements

Common Requirements for USA Study

Requirements vary depending on institution, program level, academic profile, funding, and visa situation. Students should always check official sources and receive personalised counselling.

Valid passport
Academic certificates and transcripts
English test result such as IELTS, TOEFL, PTE or Duolingo, if required
Statement of Purpose / essays
Recommendation letters, if required
Financial documents and sponsorship evidence
I-20 from the institution
DS-160, SEVIS fee and visa interview preparation
