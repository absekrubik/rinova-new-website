Application Process

Step-by-Step Japan Process

01

Counselling & Profile Assessment

Review academic background, Japanese/English level, budget, and future goals.

02

Institution Selection

Choose suitable universities, language schools, or vocational colleges.

03

Application Submission

Prepare academic documents, SOP, financial documents, and application forms.

04

Offer Letter & COE

Receive admission documents and Certificate of Eligibility (COE).

05

Visa Application

Apply for Japanese student visa with required documentation.

06

Pre-departure Support

Guidance regarding accommodation, travel, airport arrival, and student life in Japan.
