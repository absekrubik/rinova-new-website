About PTE

What is PTE?

PTE Academic is a computer-based English language test accepted by many universities, institutions, and visa pathways around the world. It assesses Speaking, Writing, Reading, and Listening skills.

Computer-Based Test

PTE is delivered on computer, making digital practice, typing speed, and headset-based speaking important.

Fast Results

PTE results are usually released quickly, which can help students move faster with admission or visa planning.

Accepted Globally

PTE is accepted by many universities and institutions in Australia, Canada, UK, USA, and other countries.

Score-Focused Practice

Structured preparation helps improve fluency, accuracy, timing, typing, listening, and overall exam confidence.

Common PTE Score Expectations

PTE Scores for Study Abroad

PTE requirements vary by country, university, course level, and visa pathway. Students should always check official institution and visa requirements.

Australia: commonly around PTE 50 to 65 depending on program
Canada: requirements vary by institution and program
UK: many institutions accept PTE with course-specific scores
USA: acceptance and score requirements vary by university
Higher-level programs may require higher scores
Some institutions may require minimum communicative skill scores
