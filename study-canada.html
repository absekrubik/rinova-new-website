Application Process

Step-by-Step Canada Process

01

Counselling & Profile Review

Review academic background, English level, budget, and future career goals.

02

Institution Selection

Choose suitable universities or colleges based on profile and study goals.

03

Application Submission

Prepare academic documents, SOP, and submit applications.

04

Offer Letter & Tuition Deposit

Receive offer letter and complete required tuition payment.

05

Study Permit Application

Prepare financial documents and apply for Canadian study permit.

06

Pre-departure Support

Guidance regarding travel, accommodation, airport arrival, and student life in Canada.

Common Requirements for Canada Study

Valid passport
Academic certificates and transcripts

Financial documents and sponsorship evidence

Medical examination if applicable
