About Stock Market

Why Learn the Nepali Stock Market?

The Nepali stock market can be a valuable learning area for students, professionals, business owners, and new investors who want to understand how shares, IPOs, market cycles, and investment decisions work.

Understand NEPSE

Learn how Nepal Stock Exchange works, how prices move, and how market trends are formed.

IPO & Mero Share

Understand IPO application, Mero Share, CRN, DEMAT, and basic investor setup.

Portfolio Management

Learn how to track holdings, calculate profit/loss, diversify, and manage risk.

Risk Awareness

Understand market risk, emotional trading, rumours, overtrading, and safe investing habits.

Stock Market Modules

What You Will Learn

This course focuses on practical understanding of Nepal’s stock market, IPO process, secondary trading, portfolio tracking, basic analysis, and responsible investment behaviour.

Stock Market Basics

Learn shares, stock exchange, listed companies, sectors, brokers, investors, and market participants.

NEPSE Overview

Understand NEPSE index, sub-indices, market capitalization, turnover, market depth, and price movement.

Mero Share & DEMAT

Learn DEMAT account, Mero Share, CRN number, IPO application, portfolio viewing, and share transfer basics.

IPO & FPO

Understand IPO, FPO, rights shares, bonus shares, allotment, refunds, and primary market basics.

Secondary Market

Learn broker account, buy/sell orders, TMS basics, order types, settlement, and trading workflow.

Risk Management

Learn stop-loss thinking, diversification, capital protection, emotional control, and investment discipline.
Analysis & Practice

Learn Practical Market Analysis

Students will learn simple tools and practical methods to understand company performance, market movement, and investment decisions.

Fundamental Analysis

Learn EPS, PE ratio, book value, dividend history, paid-up capital, reserves, and company performance basics.

Technical Basics

Understand candlestick basics, trend lines, support, resistance, volume, and simple chart reading.

Market News Reading

Learn how to read announcements, dividends, financial reports, NRB updates, and company notices carefully.

Portfolio Tracking

Track holdings, average cost, realised/unrealised profit, sector allocation, and investment performance.

Investor Psychology

Understand fear, greed, herd behaviour, rumours, panic selling, and disciplined decision-making.

Common Mistakes

Avoid overtrading, borrowing to invest blindly, following rumours, poor diversification, and emotional decisions.
Learning Pathway

Step-by-Step Stock Market Learning Process

01

Understand Share Market Basics

Learn what shares are, how companies raise capital, how investors participate, and how NEPSE operates.

02

Set Up Investor Knowledge

Understand DEMAT, Mero Share, CRN, broker account, TMS, IPO application, and secondary market access.

03

Learn Primary and Secondary Market

Study IPO, FPO, rights shares, bonus shares, buying, selling, settlement, and trading workflow.

04

Analyse Companies and Market Trends

Learn basic fundamental indicators, simple chart reading, news interpretation, and sector movement.

05

Practice Portfolio Management

Track holdings, calculate profit/loss, understand diversification, and review investment performance.

06

Build Responsible Investment Habits

Learn risk control, emotional discipline, long-term thinking, and how to avoid common market mistakes.
