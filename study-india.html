Why India?

Why Students Choose India

Affordable Education

India offers comparatively affordable tuition fees and living costs, making it accessible for many Nepalese students.

Medical & Health Programs

India is widely known for medical, nursing, pharmacy, public health, and healthcare-related programs.

Engineering & IT Pathways

Students can explore strong engineering, computer science, IT, and technology-focused education.

Cultural Familiarity

Nepalese students often find India comfortable due to cultural closeness, easy travel, and regional familiarity.

Admission Process

Step-by-Step India Process

01

Counselling & Profile Assessment

Review academic background, preferred course, budget, location preference, and career goals.

02

Institution & Course Selection

Choose suitable universities, colleges, or professional programs based on eligibility and future plans.

03

Application Preparation

Prepare academic documents, identity documents, application forms, and course-specific requirements.

04

Entrance / Eligibility Review

For some programs, students may need entrance exams, eligibility verification, or additional institutional requirements.

05

Admission Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and complete required fee or enrolment steps.

06

Travel & Pre-departure Support

Guidance regarding travel, accommodation, student registration, and adjustment to student life in India.
