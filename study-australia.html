Application Process

Step-by-Step Process

01

Counselling & Profile Assessment

We review your academic background, English level, budget, and future goals.

02

Course & Provider Selection

Select CRICOS-registered courses and institutions suitable for your goals.

03

Application & Offer Letter

Prepare application documents and apply for admission.

04

Genuine Student & Financial Documents

Prepare strong documentation, financial evidence, and Genuine Student statement.

05

COE & Visa Lodgement

After meeting offer conditions, receive COE and lodge Student Visa Subclass 500.

06

Pre-departure Support

Receive travel, accommodation, airport, and settlement guidance.

Requirements

Common Requirements for Australia

Requirements may vary depending on course, provider, qualification level, applicant profile, and visa assessment. Students should always check official information and receive personalised advice.

Valid passport
Academic certificates and transcripts
English test result such as IELTS/PTE, if required
Offer letter and Confirmation of Enrolment
Genuine Student statement and supporting documents
Financial capacity evidence
Overseas Student Health Cover
Health and character requirements
