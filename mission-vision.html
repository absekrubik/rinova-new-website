Mission & Vision

Building Futures Through Guidance, Skills & Global Education

At Rinova Education, our purpose is to help students discover better opportunities, prepare with confidence, and move forward with the right academic and career direction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture students with honest counselling, practical learning, and reliable academic support so they can confidently pursue international education and career-focused opportunities.

We aim to provide transparent guidance, professional training, and personalised support that helps every student make informed decisions about their future.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of Nepal’s most trusted and modern education platforms, recognised for quality counselling, skill development, and student success.

We envision Rinova as a place where students do not only plan to study abroad, but also grow their confidence, skills, and global mindset.

Message from Chairperson

Guiding Students Towards a Brighter Tomorrow

Welcome to Rinova Education. Our institute was established with a clear purpose — to support students with genuine guidance, practical learning, and trusted education services.

We understand that choosing a course, destination, university, or career pathway is one of the most important decisions in a student’s life. Therefore, our team is committed to providing transparent counselling, accurate information, and continuous support throughout the student journey.

At Rinova, we believe education is not only about admission or visa processing. It is about helping students become confident, skilled, and prepared for real opportunities in Nepal and abroad.

Milan Shrestha

Chairperson, Rinova Education
