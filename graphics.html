About Graphic Design

Why Learn Graphic Design?

Graphic design is one of the most useful creative skills for students, businesses, freelancers, content creators, marketers, and future digital professionals.

Creative Career Skill

Develop design skills useful for branding, marketing, content creation, and freelancing.

Marketing Designs

Create posters, banners, flyers, brochures, ads, and campaign materials.

Social Media Content

Design attractive posts, reels covers, stories, thumbnails, and online promotions.

Freelance Opportunities

Use design skills for freelance work, business promotion, and portfolio building.

Software You Will Learn

Photoshop, Illustrator and Canva

This course focuses on practical design work using three popular design tools for photo editing, vector graphics, branding, and fast content creation.

Adobe Photoshop

Learn photo editing, background removal, retouching, colour correction, poster design, social media graphics, and image composition.

Adobe Illustrator

Learn vector design, logo creation, icons, typography, shapes, brand elements, and professional illustration basics.

Canva

Create fast and attractive designs using templates, brand kits, social media layouts, presentations, and marketing graphics.
Graphic Design Modules

What You Will Learn

Learn design principles, software tools, creative workflows, and practical design projects suitable for real business and social media use.

Design Fundamentals

Colour theory, typography, layout, spacing, alignment, contrast, and visual hierarchy.

Photo Editing

Image correction, cropping, resizing, background removal, retouching, and effects.

Logo & Branding

Create logos, brand colours, typography systems, icons, and simple brand identity.

Social Media Design

Design Facebook/Instagram posts, stories, ads, thumbnails, reels covers, and banners.

Print Design

Create flyers, brochures, certificates, ID cards, business cards, and posters.

Portfolio Projects

Build a collection of design works for jobs, freelancing, and client presentation.
Learning Pathway

Step-by-Step Graphic Design Learning Process

01

Understand Design Principles

Learn colours, fonts, layout, spacing, balance, and how good designs communicate clearly.

02

Start with Photoshop

Practice photo editing, background removal, poster design, image effects, and social media graphics.

03

Learn Illustrator for Vector Design

Create logos, icons, shapes, illustrations, typography designs, and brand elements.

04

Use Canva for Fast Content Creation

Create templates, posts, presentations, banners, flyers, and quick marketing designs.

05

Create Real Marketing Designs

Design social media campaigns, posters, flyers, brand posts, and promotional materials.

06

Build Your Design Portfolio

Prepare sample works to showcase your skills for clients, jobs, internships, and freelancing.

Skills Outcome

After Completing the Course

Students will be able to create attractive designs for social media, branding, print materials, marketing campaigns, and basic freelance projects.

Edit photos and create visual compositions using Photoshop
Design logos, icons, and vector graphics using Illustrator
Create fast social media and marketing designs using Canva
Prepare posters, flyers, banners, business cards, and certificates
Understand colour, typography, layout, and branding basics
Build a beginner-level graphic design portfolio
