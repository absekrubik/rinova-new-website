About IELTS

What is IELTS?

IELTS is one of the world’s most recognised English language tests for study, work, and migration. It assesses Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking skills.

IELTS Academic

Suitable for students applying to universities, colleges, and higher education institutions abroad.

IELTS General

Commonly used for migration, work pathways, and general international English language requirements.

Accepted Worldwide

IELTS is accepted by many institutions and organisations in Australia, Canada, UK, USA, New Zealand, and more.

Score Improvement

Structured preparation helps improve fluency, accuracy, vocabulary, grammar, confidence, and test performance.

Common IELTS Score Expectations

IELTS Scores for Study Abroad

IELTS requirements vary by country, institution, course level, and visa pathway. Students should always check official course requirements.

Australia: commonly around 6.0 to 6.5 depending on program
Canada: commonly around 6.0 to 6.5 depending on institution
UK: commonly starts from around 6.0 depending on course
USA: varies widely by university and program
Higher-level programs may require higher band scores
Some institutions may require minimum scores in each module
