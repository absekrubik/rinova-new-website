Application Process

Step-by-Step Korea Process

01

Counselling & Profile Assessment

Review academic background, Korean/English level, budget, and future goals.

02

Institution & Program Selection

Choose suitable universities, language programs, or degree options based on your profile.

03

Application Preparation

Prepare academic documents, SOP, financial documents, recommendation letters, and forms if required.

04

Offer Letter & Tuition Process

Receive admission outcome and complete tuition or confirmation steps required by the institution.

05

Student Visa Application

Prepare documents and apply for the relevant Korean student visa category.

06

Pre-departure Support

Guidance regarding accommodation, travel, airport arrival, student life, and cultural preparation.
